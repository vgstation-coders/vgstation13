N3X15 updated:

  • All objects caught in explosions or in Singuloth's maw are now deleted using a new technique. This should make explosions and singulo releases nearly lagless.
  • Xenoarch updated from Bay.
  • Ghosts can no longer interact with an object if it's blessed with holy water.
  • Ghosts have a cooldown before they can interact with light switches.
  • A new streaming audio system has been stolen from D2K5's old code and reimplemented.
  • Added Chariot spell to Wizards.
  • Added a huge new variety of things to the asteroid: Caves, mobs, Gibtonite from /tg/, custom dungeon generation, wider variety of dungeons.
  • Added Go-Karts, sprited by Rei1226.
  • Ghosts can click on posibrains or the sign up link to register for being a posibrain. Golem runes and pAIs work in a similar fashion.
  • Plant pods now spawn Dionae.
  • Clicking on a standing human, alien, or monkey will allow you to hug them. Can spread diseases and trigger farts.
  • Added Atmospherics Automation Console, which allows basic automation of many atmospherics-related systems, such as injectors, vent pumps, and digital valves. Uses a visual scripting language.
  • Display case can now be opened with the captain's ID, setting the fingerprint (empty hand), and using the fingerprint (empty hand). After the gun is out, you can insert a new item, and lock the display case by swiping your ID. Breaking it requires harm intent, otherwise it's the same as the old display case.
  • Display cases are constructable by using a glass sheet on an empty machine frame, then inserting airlock controller board and screwdrivering it. Permissions set on airlock controller will be set on the display case.
  • Injector flow rate is now controllable from General Air Control consoles.
  • Added planning frame to RD's office. Allows you to plan out a lawset by inserting boards into it, examine to preview, right-click > dry run to check for common mistakes. Runs all laws at once when inserted an upload.
  • Clicking on the name of a design in a circuit imprinter or protolathe will build it immediately.
  • Genetics has a vast new array of powers and disabilities, and nearly twice as many blocks to contend with.
  • Bartender and Detective now start off with the Sober gene.
  • Overdoses treatable with Activated Charcoal, available via Biogenerator. Must be ground and then injected or ingested.
  • People who fail to spawn with their items (e.g. fatties) will now get them in a plastic bag held in their hand.
  • Due to abuse, mice are now killed by tasers and other projectiles.
  • Magboots can be stored in Suit Storage Units.
  • Assistant jumpsuit is now fat-compatible.
  • Jumpsuits with missing sprites show up with an ugly black and purple checkerboard pattern.
  • Traitors should no longer be asked to steal things their job provides easy access to.
  • MoMMIs no longer drop their stunprods when emagged.

Yota updated:

  • Photograph now rendered crisp and clean, so that we may enjoy them in their 0.009 megapixel goodness.
  • Cameras should now capture more of what they should, and less of what they shouldn't.

2014.01.15

Iamgoofball updated:

  • Added catwalks.

JohnFuklaw updated:

  • Enlarged the Vox Skipjack.

N3X15 updated:

  • Ghost interaction re-added.
  • Merged in /tg/'s recode of wire hacking.
  • Added a TEG to the left side of engineering.
  • Freezers/Heaters can be rotated.
  • Added a bunch of new posters from Bay.
  • Added telecrystals from /tg/, can be used to boost the power of a telepad past jamming by opening it with a screwdriver and jamming in a crystal.
  • Fire extinguishers can now be filled with any chemical. Wrench to open cap, dump contents onto floor, fill with beaker, wrench to close. Can also be filled by any reagent dispenser (virus food, fueltanks, pepper spray, water tanks, etc).
  • Standardized how vault floor tiles work, made some new dark floor tiles with department colors.
  • Build catwalks by using 2 rods on a lattice. Remove with a screwdriver.
  • Newer /tg/ slime recipes work properly.
  • R&D consoles have different boards for each access level, will pop out the appropriate board when deconstructed.
  • Detective's scanner works properly on bloodstains/other crap on the floor.
  • Conveyor belt switches have a 30-tile radius and no longer work across z-levels.
  • Mousetraps will now send pulses to assembly devices correctly.
  • Silver slimes now produce drinks upon injection with water.
  • Flames now properly extinguish.
  • Bats are now a swarm of bats.
  • Slimes split from a killed adult will be able to understand humans.
  • MoMMIs that lose charge or get their cells removed will no longer teleport tools into walls.
  • Secure lockers and fridges cannot be opened while welded anymore.
  • C4 on a locker/crate will dump its contents instead of deleting it entirely.
  • Posters can no longer be duplicated with wirecutters.
  • Bees will die to vacuums, hot temperatures.
  • Brain borers are no longer dense (you will move "over" them instead of into them).
  • Freezers and Fridges are now an Indiana Jones-Certified bomb-proof shelter.

Pomf123 updated:

  • Added an [OPEN] link for AI's to open doors without interrupting their current view.
  • Added an assistant limiter, enablable through the config.
  • Added a new preference setting: Randomized Character Slot. If it is set to 'Yes' it will take all your properly setup character slots and pick one to use upon round start. This has no effect for late joining however.
  • Braindamage no longer message up hivemind usage (':g absorbing captain' doesn't show up as ';g absorbing captain')
  • Fixed ryetalin making powers no longer manifesting after usage.
  • Fixed stripping people and picking up items through dense objects
  • Hopefully fixed everlasting plasma overlays.
  • Fixed jacket/coat storage and holsters not being checked for theft objectives.
  • Fixed vampires not being hurt by ingested holy water.
  • Fixed ATM crash exploit.
  • Fixed quick item equip not updating hair leaving you with a bald head.
  • Explosion optmizations.
